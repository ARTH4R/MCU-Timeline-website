MCU TIMELINE
Timeline Diffing in Ocean All Alone More
Marvel Cinematic Universe
Phase 1 - 3
Scroll Down To Start ↡
Phase 1
2008
Iron Man
  • Introduces Tony Stark, who becomes Iron Man
  • Nick Fury approaches Stark about the Avengers Initiative
2008
The Incredible Hulk
  • Introduces Bruce Banner/Hulk
  • General Ross pursues Hulk setting up future conflicts
2010
Iron Man 2
  • Introduces Black Widow and James Rhodes
  • Hints at the formation of the Avengers
2011
Thor
  • Introduces Thor Loki and Asgardian mythology
  • Sets up the cosmic aspect of the MCU
2011
Captain America: The First Avenger
  • Introduces Captain America and the Tesseract (Space Stone)
  • Establishes Hydra as a major threat
2012
The Avengers
  • Brings together Iron Man Captain America Thor Hulk Black Widow and Hawkeye as the Avengers
  • Loki allies with Thanos setting up the Infinity Saga
Phase 2
2013
Iron Man 3
  • Tony Stark faces the Mandarin and deals with PTSD after the events of The Avengers
  • Introduces Extremis technology
2013
Thor: The Dark World
  • Thor battles Malekith, the Dark Elf and deals with the consequences of Jane Foster's exposure to the Aether (Reality Stone)
  • Sets up the convergence of the Nine Realms
2014
Captain America: The Winter Soldier
  • Steve Rogers discovers Hydra's infiltration of S.H.I.E.L.D. and faces the Winter Soldier who is revealed to be Bucky Barnes
  • Establishes the collapse of S.H.I.E.L.D. and its impact on the MCU
2014
Guardians of the Galaxy
  • Introduces the Guardians—Star-Lord Gamora Drax Rocket and Groot
  • Expands the cosmic side of the MCU and introduces the Orb (Power Stone)
2015
Avengers: Age of Ultron
  • The Avengers battle Ultron, an AI created by Tony Stark, and meet Wanda Maximoff/Scarlet Witch and Pietro Maximoff/Quicksilver
  • Sets up tensions within the team and the Vision's introduction
2015
Ant-Man
  • Introduces Scott Lang as Ant-Man and Hank Pym's Pym Particles technology
  • Establishes the Quantum Realm as an important concept
Phase 3
2016
Captain America: Civil War
  • The Avengers are divided over the Sokovia Accords, leading to a conflict between Team Cap (Steve Rogers) and Team Iron Man (Tony Stark)
  • Introduces T'Challa/Black Panther and Peter Parker/Spider-Man into the MCU
2016
Doctor Strange
  • Introduces Dr. Stephen Strange and explores the mystical side of the MCU, including the Sorcerer Supreme and the Multiverse
  • Sets up the Time Stone's importance
2017
Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2
  • Continues the adventures of the Guardians and explores Peter Quill's parentage
  • Introduces Ego the Living Planet and Mantis
2017
Spider-Man: Homecoming
  • Follows Peter Parker's life as Spider-Man, mentored by Tony Stark
  • Introduces Adrian Toomes/Vulture as the main antagonist
2017
Thor: Ragnarok
  • Thor faces off against Hela, the Goddess of Death, and deals with the destruction of Asgard
  • Introduces Valkyrie and sets up Thor's journey leading into "Avengers: Infinity War"
2018
Black Panther
  • Explores the nation of Wakanda and follows T'Challa's rise as king and Black Panther
  • Introduces Erik Killmonger as a compelling antagonist
2018
Avengers: Infinity War
  • The Avengers, along with the Guardians of the Galaxy and other allies, face Thanos in his quest for the Infinity Stones
  • Ends with Thanos successfully acquiring all six Infinity Stones and wiping out half of all life in the universe
2018
Ant-Man and The Wasp
  • Follows Scott Lang/Ant-Man and Hope van Dyne/Wasp as they team up to rescue Janet van Dyne from the Quantum Realm
  • Deals with the consequences of "Captain America: Civil War"
2019
Captain Marvel
  • Introduces Carol Danvers/Captain Marvel and explores her origin story set in the 1990s
  • Ties into the larger cosmic mythology of the MCU
2019
Avengers: Endgame
  • The Avengers embark on a time heist to gather the Infinity Stones and undo Thanos' snap
  • Features the climactic battle against Thanos and the sacrifice of Tony Stark/Iron Man
2019
Spider-Man: Far From Home
  • Deals with the aftermath of "Avengers: Endgame" and follows Peter Parker's struggle to fill Iron Man's shoes
  • Introduces Mysterio as a villain
